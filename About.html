About GUESS India

Welcome to GUESSS India, a trailblazing chapter in the Global University Entrepreneurial Spirit Students' Survey (GUESSS). GUESSS India is not just a survey; it's an opportunity to amplify India's voice on the global stage, contributing to a collective understanding of student entrepreneurship that transcends borders. Together, we embark on a journey of discovery, empowerment, and shaping the future of innovation. At the heart of GUESSS India lies an intricate exploration of research topics that unravel the complex web of factors influencing student entrepreneurship. We delve into aspects such as entrepreneurial intentions, attitudes, motivations, and challenges faced by students. Our aim is not only to understand the current landscape but also to contribute to a deeper comprehension of how educational institutions, cultural factors, and economic conditions shape the entrepreneurial spirit. Data forms the bedrock of GUESSS India's research journey. By gathering information from students across various universities in India, we not only paint a vivid picture of the present but also facilitate longitudinal analysis that unveils trends and transformations over time. This commitment to data-driven insights equips us with the tools to identify opportunities, challenges, and best practices that can foster a thriving ecosystem for student entrepreneurs.


Mission and Objectives

GUESSS India aligns with the overarching goals of the Global University Entrepreneurial Spirit Students' Survey (GUESSS) project. Our mission is threefold: to delve into the entrepreneurial mindset of students, to unearth the factors that influence their entrepreneurial choices, and to contribute to a global understanding of student entrepreneurship. By painting a comprehensive picture of student-driven innovation, we're fostering an environment ripe for creativity and growth.


About GUESS Global

As the largest international research initiative focused on student entrepreneurship, GUESSS has been shaping the landscape since its inception in 2003. It's a story of collaboration, exploration, and the pursuit of understanding the dynamic world of student-led innovation and enterprise. The GUESSS saga began in 2003 through the partnership of two distinguished Swiss institutions, the University of St. Gallen and the University of Bern. This collaborative endeavor embarked on a mission to unravel the intricacies of student entrepreneurship, igniting a global movement that has now spanned over two decades. Since its inception, GUESSS has journeyed through nine data collection waves, leaving behind a trail of insights and revelations. Each wave has contributed to a deeper understanding of student entrepreneurship, guiding academic discourse and inspiring innovative policies around the world. 2021 marked a milestone as GUESSS encompassed 58 countries in its survey, illustrating its global reach and impact. In 2023, the GUESSS spotlight turns to India. Indian universities will join the survey for the very first time, bringing the nation's rich entrepreneurial spirit to the forefront of global exploration.
