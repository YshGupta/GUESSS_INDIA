Dr. Puran Singh

Country Delegate, GUESSS India

Dr. Puran Singh is an accomplished academic and researcher specializing in finance and entrepreneurship. He currently serves as an Associate Professor at the School of Management in IIT Mandi, Himachal Pradesh, where he has been actively contributing to the field since 2015. His research in the area of entrepreneurship focuses on new venture creation dynamics, student entrepreneurship, and incubation ecosystem. He has developed and taught courses in the areas of entrepreneurship at undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral levels.

Dr Puran has also made significant contributions to the field of entrepreneurship beyond academia. He is the faculty in-charge and one of the Directors of IIT Mandi Catalyst, the first technology business incubator in Himachal Pradesh. He has led the setting up and development of Catalyst since its establishment in 2016. He has acted as an enabler of the startup ecosystem in the state, has powered new venture journey over 350 startups, and brought tens of startup and industry experts /investors to the state. He has been working in tandem with the government agencies at the state and central government and the larger startup ecosystem in the country. He is on the Governing Board of the Indian STEPS and Incubatorsâ€™ Association (ISBA). He also serves as a member on the CII Northern Region Committee on Startup & Entrepreneurship, and as the co-convener on the CII HP Panel on Education & Startups. He has been invited to speak about the state's startup ecosystem at various events and platforms.

Guiding the Path to Innovation

Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell (MoE's Innovation Cell)

As national partners of GUESSS 2023, the Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell (MoE's Innovation Cell) and the Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) collaborate to foster a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship among students. Collaborating with IICs, which act as innovation hubs within educational institutions, GUESSS gains insights from the grassroots level, contributing to a comprehensive understanding of student entrepreneurship from more than 7000 higher educational institutes across eight zones in India. Together, they empower India's aspiring entrepreneurs and drive innovation-led growth.
