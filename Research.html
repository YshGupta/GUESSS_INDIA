Expected Research Output

The culmination of our research journey is a series of academic and practitioner-oriented publications. These publications are more than ink on paper; they're catalysts for dialogue, strategies, and transformation. Immerse yourself in the depth of knowledge stemming from GUESSS India's research. Each publication becomes a stepping stone in the journey toward a more vibrant, innovative, and supportive environment for student entrepreneurship.

  • Maiden India Dataset

    Data collection forms the bedrock of GUESSS India's research journey. By gathering information from students across various universities in India, GUESSS India will create the maiden dataset to unravel current trends and future transformations in student entrepreneurial spirit.

  • Country Report

    Anticipate the unveiling of a compelling narrative within the GUESSS India Country Report. This meticulously curated document will be a testament to the collective effort of Indian universities, shedding light on the intricate tapestry of student entrepreneurship. Beyond data, this report will tell stories of innovation, resilience, and vision. Its significance extends beyond academia, offering valuable insights for policymakers, educational institutions, and the entrepreneurial ecosystem.

  • White Papers and Academic Articles

    Data is the foundation, but knowledge is the catalyst for change. GUESSS India stands as a platform for the creation of white papers and academic articles that transcend mere numbers. By analyzing data through various lenses, we're fostering thought leadership that resonates with scholars, practitioners, and policymakers alike. Prepare to immerse yourself in a pool of insights that challenge assumptions and drive action.

Expected Research Outcomes

  • Global Visibility

    With India ranked among the top global startup hubs and its youth driving innovation, our participation in the GUESSS project will showcase the nation's entrepreneurial spirit. The abundance of startups reflects India's dynamic growth. Our vast higher education system, encompassing universities, institutes, and colleges, further nurture innovation. For the first time, GUESSS India's global dialogue will amplify the nation's contributions to student entrepreneurship, showcasing India's potential on the world stage along with 60+ countries.

  • Insights

    GUESSS India's research outcomes radiate far beyond the confines of academia. Our publications will redefine conversations, drive policy changes, and inspire action. The significance of these publications reverberates in university classrooms, startup boardrooms, and government offices.

  • Shaping Policy

    GUESSS India's insights aren't confined to academia; they extend to the corridors of policy-making. The significance lies in the way our findings shape the trajectory of student entrepreneurship policy in India. We're at the forefront of influencing policy decisions that resonate with the goals of the Institution Innovation Council (IIC) and the National Innovation and Startup Policy (NISP). Together, we're crafting an ecosystem that nurtures innovation and empowers young entrepreneurs.

  • Shaping Action

    GUESSS India isn't just a survey; it's an empowerment tool for universities. By participating, Indian universities unlock a unique lens through which they can assess and enhance their support systems for student entrepreneurship. Our findings provide a comprehensive view of strengths, weaknesses, and potential areas for growth. It's an opportunity for universities to evolve, adapt, and create an environment where entrepreneurial dreams thrive.
